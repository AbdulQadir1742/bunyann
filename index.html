Background

Sequence of Events

On April 22, 2025, following an attack on Hindu pilgrims in Pahalgam, India accused Pakistan of involvement. Pakistan offered neutral investigations, which India rejected.


On May 7, India launched "Operation Sindoor" with missile strikes on Pakistan and Azad Kashmir, resulting in 33 martyrs and over 50 wounded.


On May 9-10, India attacked Pakistani air bases, prompting Pakistan to launch Operation Bunyan Al Marsous in response.

Spiritual and National Significance

Operation Name

"Bunyan Al Marsous" means "solid cemented structure" or "firmly joined building". This name is taken from Surah As-Saff of the Holy Quran, which emphasizes the importance of believers' unity, solidarity, and standing together in the way of Allah.

National Unity

This operation brought the entire nation together on one platform. The people, armed forces, and government all united with one voice to demonstrate their resolve for Pakistan's defense.

Message

Pakistan desires peace but is prepared to go to any extent to defend itself. This operation is proof that Pakistan's armed forces have the capability to respond decisively to any form of aggression.
